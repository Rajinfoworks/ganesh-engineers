Project Overview

Ganesh Engineers applied high-performance protective coatings to a 200-meter chimney tower to withstand extreme weather conditions, chemical exposure, and high wind loads. The solution ensured long-term durability and minimal maintenance.

Key Highlights

  • Custom engineered coating system for tall structures
  • Resistance to chemical corrosion and UV degradation
  • High adherence for long-lasting protection
  • Compliant with industrial safety standards

Applications & Benefits

  • Industrial chimneys in power plants and refineries
  • Ensures structural longevity and safety
  • Reduces maintenance downtime
  • Protects against environmental hazards
