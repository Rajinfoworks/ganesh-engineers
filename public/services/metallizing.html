Overview of Metallizing

Thermal metallizing is the process of coating steel or other metal surfaces with a layer of aluminum, stainless steel, or other metals to provide superior corrosion resistance and durability. Ganesh Engineers specializes in metallizing industrial infrastructure, equipment, and machinery for long-lasting protection.

Our metallizing process ensures even coating thickness, high adhesion, and surface smoothness. It is ideal for structures exposed to extreme weather, chemical environments, or marine conditions.

Benefits of Metallizing

  • Excellent corrosion and oxidation resistance
  • Long-lasting surface protection
  • Improves durability of steel and iron structures
  • Can be combined with topcoat paint systems
  • Reduces maintenance and replacement costs

Applications of Metallizing

  • Steel bridges, pipelines, and storage tanks
  • Marine structures and offshore platforms
  • Industrial machinery and equipment
  • Power plants and heavy industry
  • Petrochemical and chemical processing plants
