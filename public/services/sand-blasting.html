Overview of Sand Blasting

Sand blasting is a high-efficiency surface treatment technique used for cleaning, rust removal, and preparing industrial surfaces for coatings. Ganesh Engineers provides professional sand blasting solutions for tanks, pipelines, machinery, and structural steel, ensuring surfaces are perfectly prepared for subsequent protective coatings.

Our sand blasting process uses specialized equipment and abrasives to remove corrosion, old paint, and surface contaminants. The result is a clean, smooth surface that enhances adhesion for powder coating, epoxy painting, and other surface protection methods.

Benefits of Sand Blasting

  • Removes rust, scale, paint, and contaminants effectively
  • Improves adhesion of coatings and extends service life
  • Prepares surfaces for high-performance finishes
  • Environmentally controlled process minimizing dust and waste
  • Cost-effective and faster than manual cleaning

Sand Blasting Process

  1. Surface Assessment: Inspecting the substrate to choose correct abrasive and technique
  2. Masking: Protecting areas not to be blasted
  3. Blasting: High-pressure abrasive cleaning to remove rust and contaminants
  4. Inspection: Measuring surface roughness and cleanliness before coating

Applications of Sand Blasting

  • Steel structures, beams, and industrial frames
  • Pipelines, tanks, and storage vessels
  • Machinery surfaces before protective coating
  • Ship hulls, bridges, and metal infrastructure
