Overview of Zinc Spray & Metallizing

Zinc spray and metallizing are thermal spray coating methods used to provide long-lasting corrosion protection to steel and iron surfaces. Ganesh Engineers delivers professional services for industrial structures, pipelines, tanks, and bridges, ensuring extended service life even under harsh environmental conditions.

The process involves spraying molten zinc or metal alloys onto prepared surfaces, forming a protective metallic layer that acts as a sacrificial coating. This prevents rust and corrosion while providing excellent adhesion for additional topcoats or paint finishes.

Benefits

  • Long-term corrosion protection for steel and iron
  • Enhances durability of industrial structures
  • Adaptable to complex shapes and large-scale surfaces
  • Compatible with topcoat paint systems
  • Cost-effective maintenance solution

Applications

  • Bridges, steel structures, and offshore platforms
  • Pipelines and storage tanks
  • Industrial machinery and equipment
  • Marine and coastal infrastructure
  • Steel fabrication and construction projects
