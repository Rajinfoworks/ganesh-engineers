Overview of Rubber Lining

Rubber lining is a specialized technique used to protect industrial equipment from chemical attack, corrosion, and abrasion. Ganesh Engineers provides rubber lining services for tanks, pipes, pumps, and other equipment in chemical, food, and water treatment industries.

We apply high-quality synthetic or natural rubber to interior surfaces of vessels or pipelines, forming a resilient barrier that extends service life and reduces maintenance.

Benefits of Rubber Lining

  • Excellent chemical and corrosion resistance
  • Protects against abrasion and mechanical wear
  • Extends equipment lifespan
  • Reduces downtime and maintenance costs
  • Custom solutions for industrial applications

Applications

  • Storage tanks for chemicals and water treatment
  • Pipes and pipelines handling corrosive fluids
  • Pumps, valves, and industrial machinery
  • Processing plants in chemical and pharma industries
  • Food and beverage industry equipment
