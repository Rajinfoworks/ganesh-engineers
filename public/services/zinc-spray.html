Overview of Zinc Spray

Zinc spray is a thermal spray coating method used to protect steel and iron surfaces from corrosion. Ganesh Engineers provides industrial-grade zinc spray solutions for bridges, pipelines, tanks, and structural steel, ensuring extended service life even in harsh environments.

The process involves spraying molten zinc onto prepared surfaces to form a protective metallic layer. This sacrificial coating prevents rust and corrosion while providing excellent adhesion for additional topcoats or painting if required.

Benefits of Zinc Spray

  • Long-term corrosion protection for steel and iron
  • Improves structural durability in industrial environments
  • Can be applied to complex shapes and large structures
  • Serves as a base for topcoat paint systems
  • Cost-effective maintenance solution

Applications of Zinc Spray

  • Bridges, steel structures, and offshore platforms
  • Pipelines and storage tanks
  • Industrial machinery and equipment
  • Marine and coastal infrastructure
  • Steel fabrication and construction projects
