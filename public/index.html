About Ganesh Engineers

Engineering excellence in surface protection since 1996

Ganesh Engineers is a specialist in industrial surface protection. We combine modern application methods, certified materials and experienced teams to deliver long-lasting protection for tanks, pipelines, structures and equipment across oil & gas, power, pharma, food processing and heavy manufacturing sectors.

Our core capabilities include Powder Coating, Sand Blasting, Epoxy Painting, Zinc Spray, Aluminum/SS Metallizing, Rubber Lining and custom protective systems. We provide both on-site and shop-based solutions tailored to each project.

Key sectors we serve:

  • Power & Energy
  • Food Processing & Pharmaceuticals
  • Heavy Engineering & Manufacturing
  • Water Treatment & Infrastructure

Why clients choose us:

  • On-site project execution capability

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the benefits of powder coating?

Powder coating provides a durable, corrosion-resistant and eco-friendly finish with low maintenance and excellent longevity.

When is sand blasting required?

Sand blasting is used for surface preparation to remove rust, old coatings and to create the right surface profile for coating adhesion.

Do you provide on-site services?

Yes, Ganesh Engineers offers both on-site and off-site services depending on project needs and access requirements.
