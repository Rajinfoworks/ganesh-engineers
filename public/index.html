About Ganesh Engineers

Engineering Excellence Since 1996

Ganesh Engineers is a trusted name in industrial surface protection, specializing in high-performance solutions that prevent corrosion and extend the life of critical infrastructure and equipment.

Services include sand blasting, powder coating, epoxy painting, and thermal spray metallizing. Our techniques withstand harsh environments, chemical exposure, and extreme temperatures.

Key sectors we serve:

  • Oil & Gas Industry
  • Power Plants & Energy Sector
  • Food Processing & Pharma Plants
  • Heavy Engineering & Manufacturing
  • Water Treatment Facilities

Quality and client satisfaction are our priorities. We use industry-standard procedures, certified materials, and experienced applicators for precise and long-lasting protection.

Our Strengths:

  • Experienced surface protection specialists
  • Customized coating and lining solutions
  • Modern equipment and application methods
  • On-site and off-site project capabilities
  • Reliable support and after-service care

Whether it’s a storage tank, pipeline, structural steel, or plant machinery — Ganesh Engineers is your reliable partner for premium surface protection that lasts.
